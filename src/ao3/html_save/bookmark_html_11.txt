       <h6 class="landmark heading">
        Summary
       </h6>
       <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
        <p>
         She wants to say
         <em>
          I didn’t want to bother you and Sophia,
         </em>
         she wants to say
         <em>
          I want to be happy for you, I really, really do,
         </em>
         she wants to say
         <em>
          is this how you felt when I was with Teddy?,
         </em>
         she wants to say
         <em>
          I’m sorry.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Instead she says, “It’s fine. It was a nice walk.” Her fingers are numb at the tips and she presses them harder into her cup, staring at the bridge of his nose to create the illusion of meeting his eyes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Or: Jake thinks she's the one, Amy pines for someone, anxiety waits for no one, and winter settles over New York.
        </p>
